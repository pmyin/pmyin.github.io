Biography

Dr. Peimeng Yin is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Texas at El Paso. He received his Ph.D. from Iowa State University in 2019.

His research focuses on numerical methods for PDEs, scientific computing, structure-preserving algorithms, and dynamical low-rank approximation.

Research Interests

  • Numerical analysis and partial differential equations
  • Finite element and discontinuous Galerkin methods
  • Structure-preserving time integration
  • Dynamical low-rank approximation
  • High-dimensional and multiscale problems
  • Scientific machine learning

Selected Work

• Positivity-preserving DG methods for Poisson–Nernst–Planck systems

• Structure-preserving FEM for Schrödinger–Poisson equations

• Dynamical low-rank methods for kinetic equations

• Neural network-enhanced adaptive FEM for PDEs
